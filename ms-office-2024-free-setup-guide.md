1. Uninstall MS office from the Laptop
2. Remove MS Office Folder from the Laptop.
3. Search for "Office Deployment Tools"
4. Open 1st Website and Click Download
5. Create a Folder in Desktop with name "MS Office 2024"
6. Open the Downloaded Deployment file from Downloads
7. Click check box, and then Click Continue.
8. Select the Folder in the desktop.
9. Now, Search for "Office Customization Tools" \& Open Website.
10. Customizations:

&nbsp;	1. Select Operating System (32/64 Bit)

&nbsp;	2. In products section:

&nbsp;		For Office Suites: Select, Office LTSC Professional Plus 2024 - Volume License.

&nbsp;	3. Select Apps Required and Off remaining (Keep only WORD, EXCEL, POWER POINT).

&nbsp;	4. Select Language to ENGLISH (UNITED STATES)

&nbsp;	5. Click EXPORT.

&nbsp;	6. Click the Radio Button for Keep Current Settings.

&nbsp;	7. Click OK.

&nbsp;	8. Click Accept Check Box. Then Click Export.

11\. Move the Customization.yml to MS Office 2024 Folder.

12\. Cut the MS Office 2024 Folder to OS Disk.

13\. Now, Open **Command Promt** as Administrator.

14\. Enter **cd "C:\\MS Office 2024"**

15\. Then enter: setup.exe /configure configuration.xml **(Configuration --> configuration)(Capital C --> small c)**

    If not works, try this also: **setup /configure configuration.xml**

16\. Now the MS Office gets Installed.

17\. Once the Setup is completed, check the Apps and Sign in (if Required).

18\. Now, Open PowerShell as Administrator.

19\. Enter: **irm https://get.activated.win | iex** and Click Enter.

20\. A new popup window will be appeared, Click 2, in another window, Click 1. Then the Installation Starts.
